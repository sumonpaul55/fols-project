The ambitious new Almendra

Designed by Patricia Urquiola, the new Almendra is now available in pre-order. An ambitious project in terms of design and materials, a synthesis of poetry and sustainability achieved with the help of sublime design and cutting edge technology. Almendra is a modular lighting system whose shapes and colors are reminiscent of the almond tree, from which it takes its name. To celebrate it, Patricia Urquiola tells us about its genesis, and talks about her vision for the future.
